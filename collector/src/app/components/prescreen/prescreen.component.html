Let's read your movie-mind!

In this game, we try to guess what movies you like

We will be asking for your opinion on
Movies
Actors
Genres
Directors
Subjects
... and use the information to guess the movies you like and don't like!

For every question, you can answer one of the following:

...if you like it
... if you neither like nor dislike, or if you simply don't know
... if you don't like it

The questions and your responses to them will be collected completely anonymously and used for research at the Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University.
